THE UNSAID

Not all words fit to their meanings, sometimes what is said is not what is meant, and what is meant is often left Unsaid.

These are opinions that were never voiced, ideas that were never brought to light, innovations that were never noticed, voices that were never heard. The realm of ‘The Unsaid’ is as vast as it is versatile and tapping into this diverse domain of unsaid experiences, of unspoken struggles of quiet determination, of unheard stories, and unexplained wonders, TEDxIGDTU brings to you a celebration of The Unsaid this 30th of October, 2020.

They say that what is left unsaid often says it all, so come join us at TEDxIGDTU2020 as we all discover our unspoken, unsaid potentials together.
We promise to provide you an experience the minds behind modern world revolutions say it all with Unsaid.

Say it with Unsaid.
